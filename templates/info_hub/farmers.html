Your mobile phone is your banking tool, payment ledger, and business hub. Protecting it means protecting your harvest earnings.

1. Mobile Banking and M-Pesa Safety

Scammers often call, pretending to be M-Pesa support or the Co-Op/Factory, trying to trick you into revealing your PIN or forwarding a confirmation message.

  • **PIN Protection:** Your Mobile PIN is a secret. Never share it with *anyone*, even if they claim to be support staff.
  • **Forwarding Scams:** If you receive a text asking you to "forward this message to confirm a transaction," it's a scam trying to authorize a payment from your phone.

2. The Urgent Co-Op/Factory Scam

Be skeptical of unexpected calls or texts about issues with your payment or yield data.

Action Item: If a call or text demands immediate action regarding your tea payment, **hang up**. Call the official, verified office number yourself to confirm the information. Do not trust the phone number or link provided in the suspicious message.

3. Protecting Your Smart Devices (IoT)

If you use smart scales, weather sensors, or other internet-connected farm equipment, they can be a weakness.

**Default Passwords:** Change the default passwords on *all* smart farm equipment and your Wi-Fi router immediately. Use the **Password Checker** tool for strong, unique passwords.
