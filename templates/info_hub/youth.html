Being online is fun, but every post creates a **digital footprint** that follows you forever. Learn how to stay safe from scams, protect your privacy, and manage your reputation.

1. The Digital Footprint Challenge üë£

Think before you post! Your personal information (where you live, where you go to school, or embarrassing content) can be seen by future employers or scammers.

  • **Key Rule:** If you wouldn't say it in front of a teacher or a future boss, don't post it.
  • **Over-Sharing:** Never post photos of your school ID, plane tickets, or documents with your full name and birthday.

2. Privacy Settings Scavenger Hunt üîí

Control who sees what! Most social media platforms default to "Public." Change them now!

Activity: Go to your settings on Instagram/TikTok and find these controls:

  1. Change your account to **Private** (only approved followers can see posts).
  2. Turn off **Location Services** for social media apps.
  3. Use a **strong, unique password** for every gaming/social account (and use the **Password Checker** tool!).

3. Spotting Gaming & V-Bucks Scams

If an offer for free game currency, skins, or accounts seems too good to be true, it's a trap. These scams try to steal your login credentials.

**Red Flag:** Never give your password to someone claiming to be a "moderator" or "security expert" over DMs. Real support staff do not ask for passwords.
